# They Are Billions Trainer 2026 

**Direct Overview**  
Trainer for They Are Billions. Real-time management of resources, units, buildings, and survival mechanics. Compatible with current 2026 updates. PC.  

Survive endless zombie hordes with ease: infinite materials, invincible colonies, and command over the apocalypse. Hotkey menu for campaign, survival, and custom maps.
